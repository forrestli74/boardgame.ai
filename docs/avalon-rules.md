# The Resistance: Avalon — Official Rules Reference

Source: [Official Rulebook PDF](https://avalon.fun/pdfs/rules.pdf) by Don Eskridge

## Objective

- **Good** (Loyal Servants of Arthur) wins by successfully completing 3 of 5 Quests.
- **Evil** (Minions of Mordred) wins if 3 Quests fail, OR by assassinating Merlin after Good completes 3 Quests, OR if 5 consecutive team proposals are rejected in a single round.

|---------|---|---|---|---|---|----|
| Good    | 3 | 4 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 6  |
| Evil    | 2 | 2 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4  |

### Fails Required to Fail a Quest

| Players    | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 | 9 | 10 |
|------------|---|---|---|---|---|----|
| 1st Quest  | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1  |
| 2nd Quest  | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1  |
| 3rd Quest  | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1  |
| 4th Quest  | 1 | 1 | **2** | **2** | **2** | **2**  |
| 5th Quest  | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1  |

**4th Quest special rule:** In games of 7+ players, the 4th Quest (and only the 4th Quest) requires **at least 2 Fail cards** to be a failed Quest (instead of the usual 1).

## Characters

### Required (always in game)

- **Merlin** (Good) — Knows the identities of all Evil players (except Mordred, if in play). Must hide his identity to avoid assassination.
- **Assassin** (Evil) — If Good completes 3 Quests, the Assassin names one Good player as Merlin. If correct, Evil wins instead.

### Optional

- **Percival** (Good) — Knows who Merlin is (but if Morgana is in play, sees both Merlin and Morgana without knowing which is which).
  - *Note: For 5-player games, add either Mordred or Morgana when using Percival.*
- **Mordred** (Evil) — Unknown to Merlin. Merlin does not see Mordred during the reveal phase. Strengthens Evil.
- **Oberon** (Evil) — Does not reveal himself to other Evil players, nor learns who they are. Not a "Minion of Mordred" for reveal purposes. Strengthens Good (by weakening Evil coordination).
- **Morgana** (Evil) — Appears as Merlin to Percival. Strengthens Evil.

## Game Phases

### 1. Setup

1. Select tableau for player count.
2. Place Score Markers, Team Tokens, Quest cards adjacent to tableau.
3. Place Round marker on 1st Quest space.
4. Give each player a set of two Vote tokens (Approve + Reject).
5. Randomly select a Leader; give them the Leader token.
6. Deal Character cards face down (one per player).

### 2. Reveal Phase (Night Phase)

Players learn their roles, then the Leader reads a script to establish hidden information.

**Base script (Merlin + Assassin only):**
1. "Everyone close your eyes and extend your hand into a fist in front of you"
2. "Minions of Mordred open your eyes and look around so that you know all agents of Evil"
3. "Minions of Mordred close your eyes"
4. "All players should have their eyes closed and hands in a fist in front of them"
5. "Minions of Mordred — extend your thumb so that Merlin will know of you"
6. "Merlin, open your eyes and see the agents of Evil"
7. "Minions of Mordred — put your thumbs down and re-form your hand into a fist"
8. "Merlin, close your eyes"
9. "All players should have their eyes closed and hands in a fist in front of them"
10. "Everyone open your eyes"

**Extended script (with Mordred, Oberon, Percival, Morgana):**
- Step 2 changes to: "Minions of Mordred, **not Oberon** — open your eyes..."
- Step 5 changes to: "Minions of Mordred, **not Mordred himself** — extend your thumb..."
- After step 9, add:
  - "Merlin & Morgana — extend your thumb so that Percival may know of you"
  - "Percival, open your eyes so you may know Merlin & Morgana"
  - "Merlin & Morgana — put your thumbs down and form your hand into a fist"
  - "Percival, close your eyes"
  - "All players should have their eyes closed and hands in a fist in front of them"
  - "Everyone open your eyes"

**Information summary:**

| Role      | Knows                                              |
|-----------|----------------------------------------------------|
| Merlin    | All Evil players except Mordred                    |
| Percival  | Merlin and Morgana (cannot distinguish)            |
| Mordred   | Other Evil players (except Oberon)                 |
| Morgana   | Other Evil players (except Oberon)                 |
| Assassin  | Other Evil players (except Oberon)                 |
| Oberon    | Nothing (does not see Evil, Evil does not see him) |
| Loyal Servant | Nothing                                        |
| Minion of Mordred | Other Evil players (except Oberon)         |

### 3. Team Building Phase

1. The Leader proposes a team of the required size for the current Quest.
2. The Leader assigns Team Tokens to chosen players.
3. The Leader may include themselves. Each player may only be assigned one token.

### 4. Team Vote Phase

1. All players (including the Leader) secretly select Approve or Reject.
2. All Vote tokens are revealed simultaneously.
3. **Majority approves** → proceed to Quest phase.
4. **Tie or majority rejects** → team is rejected, Leader passes clockwise, new Team Building phase begins.
5. **Vote Track:** If 5 consecutive teams are rejected in a single round, **Evil wins immediately**.

### 5. Quest Phase

1. Each Team member receives a set of Quest cards (Success and Fail).
2. Each Team member secretly selects one Quest card and plays it face down.
3. Cards are collected, shuffled, then revealed.
4. **Quest succeeds** if all cards are Success.
5. **Quest fails** if 1 or more Fail cards are played.
   - Exception: **4th Quest in 7+ player games** requires **2+ Fail cards** to fail.
6. **Good players must play Success.** Evil players may play either Success or Fail.
7. Place appropriate Score marker (blue=success, red=fail) on the tableau.
8. Advance Round marker. Leader passes clockwise. Vote Track resets.

### 6. Game End & Assassination

The game ends immediately when:
- 3 Quests succeed → proceed to Assassination phase
- 3 Quests fail → **Evil wins**
- 5 consecutive team rejections → **Evil wins**

**Assassination:** If Good completes 3 Quests, Evil players discuss (without revealing Character cards) and the Assassin names one Good player as Merlin. If correct, **Evil wins**. If incorrect, **Good wins**.

## Optional Rules

### Targeting Variant

- Players may complete Quests in any order (not just sequential).
- The Leader chooses both the team AND which Quest to attempt.
- Team size must match the chosen Quest's requirement.
- A Quest may not be attempted a second time once completed.
- The 5th Quest cannot be attempted until at least 2 other Quests have been completed successfully.
- The 4th Quest 2-Fail rule still applies for 7+ players.

### Lady of the Lake

- Best for games of 7+ players. Strengthens Good.
- At game start, give the Lady of the Lake token to the player to the Leader's right.
- Immediately after the **2nd, 3rd, and 4th** Quest is resolved, the Lady holder chooses one player to examine.
- The examined player passes a Loyalty card matching their true loyalty to the Lady holder.
- The Lady holder may discuss what they learned but **cannot reveal the Loyalty card**.
- The examined player receives the Lady of the Lake token.
- A player who has held the Lady cannot be examined by it again.
- The Lady is used only **3 times** in the game.

## Recommended Role Setups by Player Count

The official rulebook only requires Merlin + Assassin; all other roles are optional. The table below reflects community consensus from BGG, Reddit, and tournament play. See [avalon-role-setup-rationale.md](avalon-role-setup-rationale.md) for detailed reasoning.

**The experienced setup is used by default.** Beginner setup (Merlin + Assassin only) is available as an option.

### Beginner (any count)

Merlin + Assassin only, rest are generic Loyal Servants / Minions of Mordred.

### Experienced (default)

| Players | Good | Evil |
|---------|------|------|
| 5 | Merlin, Percival, Servant | Morgana, Assassin |
| 6 | Merlin, Percival, Servant x2 | Morgana, Assassin |
| 7 | Merlin, Percival, Servant x2 | Mordred, Morgana, Assassin |
| 8 | Merlin, Percival, Servant x3 | Mordred, Morgana, Assassin |
| 9 | Merlin, Percival, Servant x4 | Mordred, Morgana, Assassin |
| 10 | Merlin, Percival, Servant x4 | Mordred, Morgana, Oberon, Assassin |

### Balance Notes

- **Percival + Morgana** are always added together — Percival without Morgana is too strong for Good.
- **Mordred** is added at 7+ — at lower counts, hiding one Evil from Merlin leaves Good with too little info (only 2 Evil total).
- **Oberon** is added at 10 (sometimes 7) — needs enough Evil teammates that losing one from coordination isn't crippling.
- **Lady of the Lake** is a popular addition at 7+ for extra investigation power.
- At **5 players**, the rulebook specifically says: add either Mordred or Morgana when using Percival.

## Key Rules for Implementation

- Character cards may never be revealed during the game.
- Players may make any claims, lie, and bluff at any time.
- The Leader can be on the team but is not required to be.
- A player may only be on a team once per proposal.
- Vote track resets after a Quest is completed (success or fail).
- Quest cards are shuffled before revealing to hide who played what.
