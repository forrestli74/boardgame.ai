# Community Growth Strategy

Ideas for making BoardGame.AI popular across different communities.

## AI/ML Research Community

- **Model leaderboard** — rank models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, Llama, etc.) on Avalon win rate, deception success, and social reasoning. Researchers love benchmarks.
- **Release game datasets** — structured reasoning traces are valuable for alignment/interpretability research. Publish on HuggingFace.
- **Write a paper or blog post** — "Can LLMs Deceive? A Social Deduction Benchmark" would get attention. Social reasoning is an under-explored eval axis.

## Board Game / Gaming Community

- **Spectator/replay mode** — let people watch AI games play out with commentary. Think "AI Twitch Plays Avalon."
- **Human-vs-AI mode** — let people test themselves against AI. This is the viral hook. Even a Discord bot integration would work.
- **Expand to more games** — Werewolf, Secret Hitler, The Resistance, Coup. Each game brings its own fan community.

## Developer Community

- **Easy game authoring** — great plugin/extension docs + a "build your own game in 30 min" tutorial.
- **Easy player authoring** — let people plug in their own models, custom prompts, or algorithmic bots.
- **GitHub Actions template** — "run 100 Avalon games on every PR to test your prompt changes." CI-driven experimentation.

## Highest-Impact Moves

Pick 2-3 to start:

1. **Model leaderboard** — drives traffic on its own. "Claude vs GPT-4 at Avalon" is a headline people click.
2. **Human-vs-AI play** (CLI or Discord bot) — turns passive interest into active engagement.
3. **HuggingFace dataset release** — gets into the ML ecosystem. Reasoning traces for social deduction don't exist yet.
4. **Shareable transcripts** — Twitter/X thread or blog showing AI reasoning. The "look at this AI lying" moment is inherently shareable.

The leaderboard + shareable transcripts combo is the fastest path to organic growth. People share surprising AI behavior.
